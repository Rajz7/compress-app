💡 Compression Tips:

  • No size reduction? If your PDF doesn't compress much and images look pixelated, it might already be compressed.
  • Missing content? Try different compression modes:
    • Images Only - Safe for documents with photos
    • Structure Only - Good for text-heavy documents
    • Full Compression - Maximum reduction but may alter formatting
  • Start with "Images Only" for the safest compression if unsure.
